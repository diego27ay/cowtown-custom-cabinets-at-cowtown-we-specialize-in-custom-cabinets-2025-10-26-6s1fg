About Cowtown Custom Cabinets

Founded in 2008, Cowtown Custom Cabinets began as a small family-owned business with a simple vision: to deliver exceptional custom cabinetry that transforms homes and businesses. Starting in a modest workshop in the heart of the Dallas-Fort Worth area, our founders recognized a gap in the market for truly personalized, high-quality cabinet solutions. Over the past 15+ years, we've grown from a two-person operation to a thriving company with a team of skilled craftsmen, designers, and installation specialists. Our journey has been marked by countless satisfied customers, beautiful transformations, and an unwavering commitment to excellence that has made us a trusted name in custom cabinetry throughout North Texas.

At Cowtown Custom Cabinets, our mission is to create stunning, functional spaces that enhance our customers' lives. We believe that every home and business deserves cabinetry that is not only beautiful but also built to last. Our core values—quality craftsmanship, customer-centric service, innovation, and integrity—guide every decision we make. We're passionate about listening to our clients' needs, pushing creative boundaries, and delivering solutions that exceed expectations. Whether you're renovating a kitchen, upgrading a bathroom, or designing a custom storage solution, we're committed to partnering with you every step of the way to bring your vision to life with exceptional quality and attention to detail.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our custom cabinet services

The timeline for our custom cabinet projects typically ranges from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the complexity of your design and the current workload. This includes the initial consultation and design phase (1-2 weeks), fabrication (2-4 weeks), and installation (1-2 weeks). We understand that timing is important, and we work diligently to meet your project deadlines. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project requirements. For rush orders, we offer expedited services with additional fees. We always keep you informed throughout every stage of the process.

We use only premium materials sourced from trusted suppliers to ensure the highest quality and durability. Our cabinet options include solid wood (oak, maple, cherry, walnut), plywood construction with veneer, and engineered wood composites. For bathroom cabinets, we offer moisture-resistant materials specifically designed to withstand humid environments. We also provide a wide variety of finishes including stains, paints, and specialty coatings. During your consultation, our design team will discuss material options that best suit your needs, budget, and aesthetic preferences. All materials come with quality guarantees and are built to last for decades with proper care.

Absolutely! Our expert designers specialize in working with existing spaces and layouts. Whether you want to maximize your current space with clever storage solutions or completely reimagine your room, we have the expertise to make it work. We'll conduct a thorough assessment of your space, taking precise measurements and considering factors like plumbing, electrical, and appliance placement. Our team can work around existing fixtures or help coordinate renovations if needed. We pride ourselves on finding creative solutions that work within your space constraints while delivering the custom look and functionality you desire. We'll present multiple design options so you can choose the best solution for your home.

We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranty coverage. All custom cabinets come with a 5-year structural warranty covering manufacturing defects, hardware, and finishes. This warranty ensures that your investment is protected against any issues related to craftsmanship or materials. Additionally, we offer extended warranty options for added peace of mind. Our warranty covers door and drawer operation, finish quality, and structural integrity. If any issues arise during the warranty period, our team will promptly address them at no additional cost. We also provide maintenance recommendations to help you get the most out of your custom cabinets and keep them looking beautiful for years to come.
